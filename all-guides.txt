GUIDE 21: Demo Guide ‚Äî How SlotStrat Works
==================================================

Category: must-hit-by
Volatility: low
Min Bet: $0.25

LOOK FOR:
Minor jackpot at 90%+ of its Must-Hit-By ceiling (e.g. $180+ out of $200 max) | Major jackpot at 90%+ of ceiling (e.g. $900+ out of $1,000 max) | Use the bet-level menu to check all denominations ‚Äî each has its own meter | Prioritize machines where BOTH Minor and Major are elevated

STOP PLAYING:
After ANY must-hit-by jackpot triggers and the meter resets to its seed value | After 60 spins without a jackpot hit (session stop-loss) | If your bankroll drops below 30x your current bet level

HOW IT WORKS:
Must-Hit-By (MHB) progressives are the most beginner-friendly advantage play category because the math is straightforward:

- The jackpot **must** trigger before reaching a posted ceiling (e.g. $200)
- As the meter climbs closer to that ceiling, the **expected value (EV) of each spin increases**
- At a high enough meter value, the EV flips positive ‚Äî meaning the jackpot payout exceeds the expected cost to trigger it

**Why this matters:**
- Previous players have already funded most of the jackpot with their losses
- You step in near the ceiling and capture the final payout at a fraction of the total cost
- The ceiling is a **hard guarantee** ‚Äî the jackpot cannot exceed it

**Example:**
- Minor jackpot must hit by $200, currently at $185
- At most $15 of play remains before it MUST trigger
- If your expected spin cost is $0.25, that is roughly 60 spins maximum
- 60 spins x $0.25 = $15 max cost for a $185+ payout

This is the core principle behind every MHB guide in the library.

---

PLAY WHEN:
<strong>Meter Requirements:</strong>
- Minor jackpot at **90%+** of Must-Hit-By ceiling
- Major jackpot at **90%+** of Must-Hit-By ceiling

<strong>Denomination Check:</strong>
