## DO NOT PLAY WHEN

- Blue pig below <strong>20</strong> free games
- Only yellow or red pigs are fat (blue must be primary trigger)
- You cannot afford <strong>$1,500+</strong> potential investment

## STOP WHEN

- Any pig feature triggers
- Bankroll loss exceeds predetermined limit
- Blue pig reaches 50+ without triggering (cut losses)

---

## COMMON MISTAKES

- Chasing pigs that "look close" (fat pigs are NOT more likely to hit)
- Playing with insufficient bankroll (can easily require $1,500+ investment)
- Expecting blue pig to trigger at 100 (100 is cap, not guaranteed trigger)
- Playing when only yellow/red are fat (blue is the primary value driver)
- Continuing after a single pig triggers without blue
