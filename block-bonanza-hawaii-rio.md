## Additional Notes

**Game Variants:**
- Hawaii: Surfer symbols = dollar symbols during free games
- Rio: Extra dollar symbols added during free games
- Same result: Higher block win frequency in bonus

**Dollar Symbol Rules:**
- Must land in sequence (reel 1 ‚Üí 2 ‚Üí 3)
- NOT scatter symbols
- Blue diamond dollars = regular dollars + random value boost
- Can land multiple dollars per reel, but need at least 1 in each of reels 1-2-3

**Volatility Warning:**
- Extremely high variance
- Difficult to land 3 dollar symbols
- No guarantee of hitting target block
- Many wins come from free games bonus
