## How The Advantage Works

Madonna: Mighty Cash features **accumulating multipliers for Mighty Cash bonus**:

**Mechanic:**
- Boost symbol lands → Multiplier above that reel +1x
- Max multiplier: <strong>8x</strong> per reel
- <strong>6+</strong> star symbols → Triggers Mighty Cash hold-and-spin bonus
- Credit prizes in bonus are pre-multiplied by reel multiplier

**Multiplier Reset:**
- After Mighty Cash bonus triggers
- A few random multipliers added to entice further play

---

## ⚠️ HIGH VARIANCE WARNING

**Bring large bankroll.**
- Advantage depends on triggering Mighty Cash bonus
- Can dig deep holes before bonus hits

---

## PLAY WHEN

<strong>Primary Requirement:</strong>
- Total of ALL multipliers ≥ <strong>21x</strong>

**How to Calculate:**
- Add all 5 reel multipliers together
- Example: 5x + 4x + 3x + 5x + 4x = 21x ✅

| Total Multipliers | Action |
|-------------------|--------|
| 21x+ | ✅ **Play** |
| Below 21x | ❌ Walk away |

---

## DO NOT PLAY WHEN

- Total multipliers below <strong>21x</strong>

---

## STOP WHEN

- Mighty Cash bonus triggers (multipliers reset)

---

## COMMON MISTAKES

- Not adding ALL 5 reel multipliers
- Playing close-but-not-quite setups (like 18x)
- Not bringing adequate bankroll

---

## Additional Notes

**Mighty Cash Bonus Details:**
- Spins = Number of stars that triggered bonus
- Fill all squares → Wheel spin for jackpots or 2x/3x multiplier
- Credit values shown are pre-multiplied

**Boost Symbol:**
- Counts towards 6 stars needed for bonus
- Appears frequently (multipliers build quickly)

**Check All Denominations:**
- Multiple bet levels available
- Check each for plays

**RTP Range:**
- 86.5% to 91.9%
