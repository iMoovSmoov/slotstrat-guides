## How The Advantage Works

Blazin' Diamond / Lightning Wilds features a **Pay Upgrade mode**:

**Mechanic:**
- Pay Upgrade symbol lands on reel 3 → Triggers <strong>3</strong> spins
- During Pay Upgrade: All 7s and BARs pay <strong>2x</strong>
- Counter decreases each spin until 0

**Why It's Valuable:**
- Nearly ALL line hits involve 7s and BARs
- 2x multiplier significantly boosts payouts
- Low volatility (predictable outcomes)

---

## PLAY WHEN

<strong>Primary Requirement:</strong>
- "Pay Upgrade Active" banner visible above pay table
- Spins remaining counter > 0

**How to Spot:**
| Indicator | Location |
|-----------|----------|
| "Pay Upgrade Active" banner | Middle of screen, above pay table |
| Spins remaining | Below the banner |
| Upbeat music | Plays when switching to active bet level |

---

## DO NOT PLAY WHEN

- No "Pay Upgrade Active" banner visible
- Spins remaining = 0
- Feature not active on any bet level

---

## STOP WHEN

- Pay Upgrade spins reach 0
- **Check all bet levels before leaving** (may be active on different denomination)

---

## COMMON MISTAKES

- Not checking all bet levels/denominations
- Missing the visual cue (banner above pay table)
- Missing the audio cue (upbeat music on active level)
- Ignoring this game entirely (many APs overlook it)

## Additional Notes

**Hidden Gem:**
- Most APs overlook this game
- Often tucked away with mechanical reel slots
- Areas most APs rarely check
- Almost always find playable setups

**How to Check All Bet Levels:**
- Tap red button at bottom center of screen
- Review all available denominations
- Look for active setups on each

**Progressive Jackpots:**
- Only available at max bet
- Chase only with large bankroll
- Unclear breakeven threshold

**Higher RTP Potential:**
- Lowest denomination: 5¢
- Also found in 25¢, $1, $2 versions
- Casinos often increase RTP on higher denominations

- Blazin' Diamond ✅
