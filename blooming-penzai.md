## How The Advantage Works

Blooming Penzai features **credit prizes and trees** above each reel:

**Mechanic:**
- Coin with value lands → Prize above that reel increases
- Prize grows → Tree turns gold
- Gold tree symbol lands (during bonus) → Win prize above
- Prize awarded → Resets to 1x bet, tree shrinks to green shrub

**Tree Progression:**
- Tree turns **fully gold** at ~<strong>20x</strong> bet value
- Fully gold = Green rim + gold orb on pot

---

## PLAY WHEN (ANY ONE)

**Option A — Total Value:**
- Total prizes above all 5 reels ≥ <strong>100x</strong> bet
- Prizes should be **spread across multiple reels** (lowers variance)

**Option B — Gold Tree Count:**
- <strong>3+</strong> fully gold trees
- Look for: Green rim + gold orb on pot

**Option C — Four Gold Trees:**
- <strong>4</strong> fully gold trees
- Almost always a play
- ❌ **EXCEPT:** All 4 recently turned gold (low prize values)

| Gold Trees | Total Value | Action |
|------------|-------------|--------|
| 0–2 | Below 100x | ❌ Walk away |
| 3+ | Any | ✅ **Play** |
| Any | 100x+ bet | ✅ **Play** |

---

## DO NOT PLAY WHEN

- Total prizes below <strong>100x</strong> bet with fewer than 3 gold trees
- Single massive prize (not spread out = high variance)
- Just triggered Blooming Gold (prizes reset)

---

## STOP WHEN

- Blooming Gold feature triggers and completes
- Trees reset to green shrubs
- Prize totals drop below threshold

---

## COMMON MISTAKES

- Chasing single large prize (need spread for lower variance)
- Not checking all 5 reels for total value
- Not recognizing fully gold tree (green rim + gold orb)
- Not checking all denominations

---

## Additional Notes

**Blooming Gold Feature:**
- Triggered by 3 vase scatter symbols
- Starts with <strong>8</strong> free spins
- +1 spin each time coin(s) land
- Coins land more frequently with larger values
- Gold tree symbols only appear during this feature

**Tree Gold Threshold:**
- Trees turn fully gold at ~<strong>20x</strong> bet
- Visual: Green rim and gold orb on pot

**Variance Warning:**
- Can take a while to trigger Blooming Gold
- Not guaranteed to win target prizes during feature
- Fairly volatile game

**Prize Reset:**
- After gold tree symbol awards prize → Resets to 1x bet
- Tree shrinks back to small green shrub

**Denominations:**
- Multiple denominations available
- Check all bet levels for plays
