## How The Advantage Works

Diamonds & Devils / Jade Monkey features **diamond collection with devil/monkey risk**:

**Mechanic:**
- Collect <strong>3</strong> diamonds above a reel → Win prize (credits or free games)
- Free games symbol lands → +<strong>8</strong> free games to that reel (max 80)
- Credit values build up as symbols land

**The Twist (Risk):**
- Devil/Jade Monkey lands → Removes <strong>1</strong> diamond
- No diamonds + Devil/Monkey → **RESETS entire reel** (loses all credits/free games)

---

## ⚠️ HIGH VARIANCE WARNING

**Discipline Required:**
- Cash out immediately when no longer +EV
- Devil/Monkey can take everything away

---

## PLAY WHEN

**Free Games Thresholds:**
| Diamonds | Free Games Required | Action |
|----------|---------------------|--------|
| 2 | <strong>8+</strong> | ✅ **Play** |
| 1 | <strong>16+</strong> | ✅ **Play** |
| 0 | <strong>24+</strong> | ✅ **Play** (always) |

**Credit Value Thresholds:**
| Diamonds | Credit Value Required | Action |
|----------|----------------------|--------|
| 2 | <strong>10x+</strong> bet | ✅ **Play** |

**How to Identify Built-Up vs Reset:**
| Number Color | Meaning |
|--------------|---------|
| Yellow | Built up (has value) |
| Silver | At reset value (minimal) |

---

## DO NOT PLAY WHEN

- Free games don't meet threshold for diamond count
- Credit values below <strong>10x</strong> bet with 2 diamonds
- Reel just reset (silver numbers)
- No diamonds and below <strong>24</strong> free games

---

## STOP WHEN

- Prize collected (3 diamonds triggered)
- Devil/Monkey resets the reel
- No longer +EV

---

## COMMON MISTAKES

- Not knowing devil/monkey removes diamonds
- Chasing after reel reset (too late)
- Not cashing out immediately when play turns -EV
- Confusing yellow (built) vs silver (reset) numbers
- Playing credit values without enough diamonds

---

## Additional Notes

**Reel Difficulty & Reset Values:**
| Reel | Trigger Difficulty | Reset Value |
|------|-------------------|-------------|
| Reel 1 | Easier | 1x bet |
| Reel 2 | Easier | 1x bet |
| Reel 3 | Medium | 2x bet |
| Reel 4 | Medium | 2x bet |
| Reel 5 | Harder | 5x bet (most desirable) |

**Free Games Bonus:**
- Very valuable (worth more than credit prizes usually)
- Devil/Monkey doesn't appear during free games
- Much easier to win prizes

**Diamond Collection:**
- Up to <strong>3</strong> diamonds can land in one reel at once
- Possible to fill completely empty reel in one spin

**Max Accumulation:**
- Free games: Up to <strong>80</strong> per reel

**Game Variants:**
- Diamonds & Devils Deluxe ✅
- Jade Monkey Deluxe ✅ (same strategy)
