## DO NOT PLAY WHEN

- Any pot shows <strong>8 free games</strong> (too high risk of hitting it)
- Fewer than <strong>4</strong> high-value pots
- Only 1 excellent pot (24 free games or wheel) with poor others
- You cannot handle high variance

---

## STOP WHEN

- Any pot triggers (all pots reset afterward)
- Bankroll loss exceeds predetermined limit

---

## COMMON MISTAKES

- Playing for a single 24 free games pot (only 20% chance to hit it)
- Not checking ALL 5 pots before playing
- Playing with ANY 8 free games pot visible
- Underestimating the variance (can take many spins to trigger)
- Continuing after a pot triggers (all pots reset)
