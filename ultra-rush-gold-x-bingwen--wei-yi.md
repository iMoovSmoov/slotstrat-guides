## How The Advantage Works

Ultra Rush Gold X features **gold scatter accumulation for bonus trigger**:

**Game Variants:**
- Ultra Rush Gold X: Bingwen
- Ultra Rush Gold X: Wei Yi

**Mechanic:**
- <strong>5</strong> scatter symbols → Bonus triggers
- Gold scatter lands → Locks for <strong>3</strong> additional spins
- Another gold lands → Spin count resets to 3
- Only need 5 ANY scatters (gold, blue bubbles, or wheel)

**Key Difference from Original:**
- "X" version: 5 scatters needed (not 6)

## COMMON MISTAKES

- Thinking you need 5 GOLD scatters (any scatters count)
- Not checking all denominations
- Missing plays by not glancing at bet pad

## Additional Notes

**Scatter Mechanics:**
- Gold scatters lock and persist
- Blue bubbles and wheel symbols also count toward 5
- Gold scatters don't block - symbols behind still evaluated
- Sub-symbol shown at bottom of gold scatter

**Bonus Extension:**
- At 0 spins, game may randomly grant 3 more spins

**Free Check Method:**
- Glance at bet pad to see collected scatters
- Tap buttons on bet pad for other denominations
- 4 denominations = Many opportunities

**RTP Range:**
- 85% to 94%
